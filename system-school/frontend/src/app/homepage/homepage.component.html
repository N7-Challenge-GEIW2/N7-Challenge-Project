About Engineering Cycle

Best Choice For your future

The engineering cycle at ENSET Mohammedia is a comprehensive educational program spanning four to five years. It combines theoretical coursework with hands-on practical training, research projects, and industrial internships in various engineering disciplines. The curriculum emphasizes practical application, problem-solving skills, and soft skills such as communication and leadership. Students are encouraged to innovate and contribute to research, preparing them for successful careers in industry, research, academia, or entrepreneurship.
